Learner-Centered Outcomes-Based Education

Through learner-centered, outcomes-based education (LC-OBE), MapúaMCM ensures that students have access to personalized learning, focused on attaining industry standards through clearly defined outcomes.

Effective Learning Spaces

By fusing innovative physical learning spaces and digital learning environments, MapúaMCM provides an inspiring environment to study, learn, and collaborate with peers. MapúaMCM provides WiFi inside the campus, cutting-edge learning resources, and a robust digital learning management system.

Mapúa Legacy

The academic excellence that Mapúa is known for is embedded in MapúaMCM’s DNA. MapúaMCM brings to Mindanao the World-Class Mapúa Education that produces graduates who are highly preferred by industries here and abroad.
